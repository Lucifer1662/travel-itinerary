## Best Time to Visit
- **Spring** (April): [Cherry blossoms](https://www.japan-guide.com/e/e6906.html)
- **Fall** (Nov): [Autumn colors](https://www.japan-guide.com/e/e6906.html)
- **Winter** (Dec-Feb): [Clearest Mt. Fuji views](https://www.japan-guide.com/e/e6906.html)
- **Avoid**: Summer (cloudy, less visibility)
